Eligibility for Beginner Level

  • No prior experience in electronics or coding is required. This course is designed for absolute beginners.
  • Recommended for students aged 8-12 years.
  • A keen interest in learning, building, and exploring new technologies is a plus!
  • Access to a computer with internet connection for online resources and simulations (if applicable).

What You'll Learn

Basic Electronics & Circuits

Understand the fundamentals of electricity, components like resistors, LEDs, and how to build simple circuits safely and effectively.

Logical Sequencing & Reasoning

Develop critical thinking skills by learning to break down problems into logical steps, essential for both coding and everyday problem-solving.

Variables & Loops

Get hands-on with fundamental programming concepts like variables to store data and loops to repeat actions, making your projects dynamic.

Circuit Design & Simulation

Learn to design your own circuits and test them virtually using simulation tools before building them physically, saving time and resources.

Block-based Coding

Dive into coding with intuitive drag-and-drop block-based environments, making complex programming concepts accessible and fun for beginners.

BBC Micro:bit Controller

Explore the versatile Micro:bit, a pocket-sized computer designed for learning, and bring your coding projects to life with its built-in sensors and LEDs.
