Innovate & Build: Intermediate Level

Advance your skills with microcontrollers, sensors, robotics, and foundational programming for real-world applications.

Explore Curriculum

Age Group

Ages 11–14 years

Duration

2 Years (Weekend-Only Program)

Sessions

8 Advanced Activities Per Month

Eligibility for Intermediate Level

  • Basic understanding of electronics or block-based coding is recommended.
  • Recommended for students aged 11-14 years.
  • A strong interest in programming, robotics, and problem-solving.
  • Access to a computer with internet connection for advanced simulations and software.

What You'll Learn

Arduino Microcontroller

Master the Arduino platform for controlling electronics, reading sensors, and building interactive projects.

Sensors & Actuators

Learn to integrate various sensors (light, temperature, distance) and actuators (motors, servos) into your projects.

MIT App Inventor

Create your own Android applications with a visual, block-based programming environment.

Ground Robotics

Design, build, and program autonomous ground robots to navigate environments and complete tasks.

Aerial Robotics (RC Plane)

Explore the principles of flight and control by building and piloting remote-controlled planes.

Python Basics

Get started with Python, a powerful and versatile programming language used in various fields.

Raspberry Pi & IoT

Utilize Raspberry Pi for more complex projects, including Internet of Things (IoT) applications.

Computer Vision Basics

Introduce yourself to how computers can "see" and interpret images and videos.

AI/ML Fundamentals

Discover the core concepts of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning through practical examples.
