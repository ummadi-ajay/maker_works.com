Master Innovators: Advanced Level

Build a strong portfolio with advanced robotics, AI, data science, and complex project development.

View Curriculum

Age Group

Ages 15+ years

Duration

2 Years (Weekend-Only Program)

Sessions

6 Capstone Projects Per Year

Eligibility for Advanced Level

  • Proficiency in foundational electronics and programming (e.g., Python, Arduino) is essential.
  • Recommended for students aged 15 years and above.
  • Demonstrated strong problem-solving skills and a passion for deep technological exploration.
  • Access to a powerful computer with stable internet, capable of running advanced development environments.
  • Prior experience with competition robotics or significant personal projects is highly beneficial.

What You'll Master

Advanced Robotics & ROS

Delve into complex robotic systems, kinematics, dynamics, and gain hands-on experience with Robot Operating System (ROS).

Embedded Systems & RTOS

Explore real-time operating systems and advanced microcontroller programming for high-performance applications.

Data Science & Analytics

Learn to collect, process, analyze, and visualize data using Python libraries for informed decision-making.

Machine Learning & AI

Implement advanced AI algorithms, neural networks, and deep learning concepts for intelligent systems.

Cloud Computing & IoT

Integrate IoT devices with cloud platforms, manage data streams, and build scalable smart solutions.

Advanced Python & C++

Deepen your programming skills with advanced Python paradigms and explore C++ for performance-critical applications.

CAD & 3D Printing

Design complex mechanical parts using CAD software and bring your designs to life with 3D printing technologies.

GUI Development

Build graphical user interfaces for your applications, enhancing user interaction and project professionalism.

Competition & Portfolio Prep

Focus on advanced competition strategies and develop a strong portfolio showcasing your high-level projects for future academic and career pursuits.
